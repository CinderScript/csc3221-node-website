My name is: Gregory Isaac Maynard

The day I had the most fun

The year was 27 ABY and the other clones and I had finished fighting in the Battle of Tirahnn. I lost many friends in the fighting. I especially miss Delta-192 whom I had become close to. We grew up in the clone factory in kamino together. Because the fighting was so hard, I think supervisor Bob felt sorry for us. Bob decided to throw us all our first birthday party. We were allowed to share a cake and they told us what a good job we were doing. It was the best day of my life.
